# Publishing to Chrome Web Store

This guide details the steps to publish the **Confluence Exporter** extension.

## 1. Preparation

The extension package has already been built for you.
- **Package File:** `confluence-exporter.zip` (Located in the project root)
- **Promo Image:** `store-assets/promo-tile.png` (Review this image before uploading)
- **Icon:** `extension/public/icons/icon128.png`

## 2. Developer Account

1.  Go to the [Chrome Web Store Developer Dashboard](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/developer/dashboard).
2.  Sign in with your Google Account.
3.  If this is your first time, you must register as a developer and pay the **$5 one-time fee**.

## 3. Uploading the Extension

1.  Click **"New Item"** in the dashboard.
2.  Drag and drop the `confluence-exporter.zip` file.
3.  The store will process the package and draft a listing.

### Graphic Assets
-   **Icon:** Upload `extension/public/icons/icon128.png`
-   **Screenshots:** You must take **at least one screenshot** of the extension in action.
    -   *Tip:* Open the extension popup on a Confluence page and take a screenshot (1280x800 recommended).
-   **Promo Tile:** Upload `store-assets/promo-tile.png` (Small promo tile).

### Privacy Policy
You may need to declare privacy practices.
-   **Data Usage:** The extension only accesses the *current tab's content* to generate the export. It does NOT collect or transmit user data to external servers (other than downloading the file to your machine).
-   **Permissions:** `activeTab`, `scripting`, `downloads`, `storage`.

## 5. Review and Publish

1.  Click **"Submit for Review"**.
2.  The review process typically takes **24-48 hours**.
3.  Once approved, your extension will be live!
